    How tree types sum to the total

    In this page we represent how the tree types sum to the total using an interactive stacked bar chart.
    Stacked Bar Chart is multiple datasets on top of each other in order to show how the larger category is divided into the smaller categories and their relations to the total amount. We decided to represent the "Others" category with grey color in order to don't distract the user from the important ones.
